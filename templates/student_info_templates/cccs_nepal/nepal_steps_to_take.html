Steps to Take

    Take SAT and TOEFL after your final exams

    • SAT requires 2 months preparation and TOEFL requires 1 month of preparation
    • Try to register for the SAT and TOEFL as early as possible. There are limited exam centers in Nepal and they may run out of seats.
    • The ideal time to take the SAT is Oct-Nov and TOEFL on May-June so that you can take it again in December if your score was not satisfactory.
    • Khan Academy has a lot of free resources to prepare for the SAT.
    • Practice and early preperation is key

    Create a common application account and fill out the details

    • Link: commonapp

    Ask your teachers for the letter of recommendation

    • A Letter of Recommendation is a document where your instructor describes your skills and potential
    • It is good to have 3 letters of recommendation
        Usually, universities asks for 2 letters of recommendation

    Draft your Personal Essay

    • Start thinking about the personal essay early
    • There are multiple personal essays to choose from, in common application
    • You should answer one of them in an elegant way
    • Start brainstorming early, consult your English teacher, and try to come up with an essay that describes you well

    Apply

    • Begin after you are done with these processes and SAT/ACT and TOEFL/IELTS
